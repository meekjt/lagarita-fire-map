Standard Embed (800x600)

<iframe src="http://localhost:8000" width="800" height="600" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="true" style="max-width: 100%; border: 1px solid #ccc; border-radius: 4px;"> </iframe>

Responsive Embed (16:9 Aspect Ratio)

<div style="position: relative; width: 100%; padding-bottom: 56.25%; height: 0; overflow: hidden;"> <iframe src="http://localhost:8000" style="position: absolute; top: 0; left: 0; width: 100%; height: 100%;" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="true"> </iframe> </div>

oEmbed Integration

For platforms that support oEmbed (like WordPress), you can simply paste the URL:

http://localhost:8000

The oEmbed endpoint is available at:

http://localhost:8000/oembed.json

Or with parameters:

http://localhost:8000/oembed.php?url=http://localhost:8000&maxwidth=600&maxheight=400
